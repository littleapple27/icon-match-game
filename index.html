Icon   Word Matching Game

You have been invited to participate to a icon  word matching game, designed as a graduate research project for the Department of Informatics at Fort Hays State University. The purpose of the study is to evaluate the degree to which flat icons have a universal meaning and can be recognized properly by users

On the following next page, you will be presented with a series of web-based application icons. For each icon, please match the icon to the word that represents the icons' meaning and/or function. Thank you for playing!
